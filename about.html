A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Mina! I'm a wedding and engagement photographer based in Zamalek – Egypt.

I’ve Always Loved Photography, But Wedding Photography Wasn’t Something I Originally Had On My Radar As A Career. My Love Of People And Learning The Stories Of Others Led Me To This Amazing Job. I Stumbled Upon A Wedding Photography Studio Looking For An Assistant And Thought It Would Be A Great Part-Time Job While I Got Used To a New City.

However, While Working In The Studio, I Connected The Dots And Realized That Photography Could Be My Career.

I’m So Happy That My Love Of Photography Has Turned Into What It Is Today, And That I Still Get To Learn The Stories Of Others, Capturing precious memories & emotions to relive love & Joy Of Ur Special Day, I’ve Been Lucky Enough To Photography Couples In Love Now For 9 Years And Couldn’t Imagine Doing Anything Else.
